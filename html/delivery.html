Information on Shipping Costs & Delivery Times

Shipping costs

All of our products will be delivered to your curbside. Please have someone present upon delivery in order to examine the crate and sign the invoice.

Delivery times

We keep in stock many of the products listed on our website; however, there may be times when a product is on back order. Back order can take up to 8 weeks, so please plan your project accordingly.

Delivery time to your destination depends on whether the item(s) is in stock and to where in the US the product is to be shipped. Please note that our warehouse is located in northern California.
