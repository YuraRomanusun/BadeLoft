The BW-01-L is a modern bathtub designed for your luxury bathroom.This freestanding tub is both peacefully tranquil and stylishly ergonomic. The mesmerizing allure of the design is met with eco-friendly and non-toxic materials so you can be assured of a safe and relaxing environment in a deep bathtub. This high quality stand alone tub delivers style and function all in one elegant package. The melodious blend of acrylic polymer and natural minerals offers an ideal solution for high quality stone bathtubs with amazing heat retention. Offering an unparalleled design, this free standing tub denotes modern composition with a classic style for a unique piece that serves as the heart and soul of the room. This luxury bathtub is 100% non-porous for great resistance to such external forces as moisture and humidity. For added ease of use, the resin tub is equipped with an overflow protection and pop-up drain plug.

Features:
  • Overflow Protection for optimal safety.
  • Pop-Up Drain Plug with your choice of finish.
  • Simple Drain installation System (View installation video below)

Stone Resin Characteristics:

Similar to Corian, stone resin is a composite of acrylic polymer and natural minerals. It is an ideal material for high quality bath fixtures as the heat retention is amazing. Our products are manufactured to be eco-friendly using non-toxic materials that are safe for our clients. It is 100% non-porous and is unaffected by external forces such as moisture and humidity. Because of difference characteristics in the matte and glossy finish there can be minimal differences in the sizes.
